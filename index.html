What is the offer amount calculator?
What to offer on a house calculator is a tool that estimates the offer value for a house.
How to calculate offer amount?
To calculate offer amount you should use the following formula:
offer amount = fair market value - cost of renovation - ((desired discount / 100) * fair market value)
